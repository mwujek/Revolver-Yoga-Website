Revolver Yoga Studio is located at 4 South 4th St. (nearly the corner of Main and 4th Ave.) in downtown Walla Walla. We encourage you to ride your bike, walk, or carpool to class, though street parking is readily available. We offer classes seven days a week. For a live schedule including teacher substitutions and cancellations, or to reserve a spot in class, click here. Most classes are 75 minutes, exceptions are noted on the schedule and in the class descriptions.
