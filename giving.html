Scholarships

It is with endless gratitude to our donor that we are able to offer scholarship for students of our studio to help attend a 200hr teacher training through YogaWorks (www.yogaworks.com). This very unique opportunity allows us to support our studentsâ€™ passion and to make sure they are receiving a solid education to grow their own teaching process. Our selection process is based upon need, passion to both learn and teach, and commitment to a yogic lifestyle. To be considered for scholarship, we require an essay articulating what you hope to gain from your experience and why you are interested in teaching yoga as well as proposal for a dana (service) project to be executed in Walla Walla at the end of your teacher training.

Please contact info@revolveryoga.com for more information.

Karma Yoga

We invite enthusiastic yoga students to work for the studio in trade for yoga classes. As part of the Revolver Yoga team, we look for dependable, consistent, good-humored yogis to join us as studio class assistants, front desk staff, and musicians looking to enhance yoga classes with live music. Please contact Courtney at courtney@revolveryoga.com for more information.
