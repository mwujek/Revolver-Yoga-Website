Jerri’s specialises her massages with a combination of deep tissue body work, Thai Yoga Massage, trigger point therapy, stretching and movement methods that will help relieve tension and pain. Reiki

Please contact Chrissy directly at (314) 601.2947 for more information and to book.

Reiki is a natural healing technique in which the practitioner directs Universal Life energy (in yoga we call it Prana) to the client using a non-invasive, stress-reducing technique. The gentle laying of hands is practiced, either directly on a patient’s fully-clothed body or resting a few inches above the body.

Reiki sessions are soothing and support the release of physical and mental tension, as well as boosting the efficiency of the immune system. On a more esoteric level, Reiki sessions increase the vibrational frequency of the body, lending to a feeling of lightness and well-being. Reiki is a different experience for each individual and each session helps to maintain your current state of wellness. Many people find relief from common aches and pains of daily life, others enjoy a quieting of the mind and a release from mental anguish while some are finally able to resolve the issues that they have been working on for years with just one session. Most people find their energy levels increase or stabilize. However, it is not necessary to believe anything at all about Reiki to derive its benefits, as many of Chrissy’s clients and yoga practitioners have experienced!

Please note: Reiki is not meant to diagnose or cure illnesses, although it may support the body in healing itself.
