Our classes are an opportunity to take your practice to a deeper level, whether you are new to yoga or have a steady practice. All of our teachers are highly trained and continue their education on a regular basis and we celebrate the diversity of our instructors’ interests and backgrounds, therefore each teacher has a unique feel and focus to their classes.

If you’re brand new to yoga (even if you are an elite athlete), start from the beginning with a Level 1 class and progress at your own pace. You can mix and match teachers and classes as much as you want - many people take a wide variety depending on what is going on physically, emotionally, and mentally at any time.

If you are running late, please be considerate of your classmates and wait outside until the class is moving. Likewise, if you must leave early please have your things up front and do so before Savasana.

    Private Yoga Sessions

    Private yoga sessions give you the opportunity to have individualized classes based solely on your needs, including specific alignment tips for your body and hands-on adjustments (if you want them). Yoga was originally passed down directly from teachers who worked one-on-one to create a practice for each student based on their health, body type and lifestyle. Your skilled Revolver Yoga teacher can do the same.

    Some Benefits of Private Yoga:

    • Schedule regular sessions at times most convenient for your busy lifestyle
    • Meet in the privacy of the studio or your own home
    • Address a specific health issue like insomnia, low back pain, chronic stress or orthopedic injuries
    • Learn postures to help step your golf game or triathlon times up a notch
    • Work towards mastering a challenging posture (like headstand or lotus)
    • Learn a custom sequence that you can practice at home

      • Please contact us for more information on pricing, teacher schedules, and creating your individualised yoga plan. Send us an email to get started.
